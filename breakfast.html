Homemade Pancake

Think o fthis super-versatile four-ingredient dry mix as your secret weapon for making fluffy pancakes and much more. Just whisk it together and store it in an airtight container for maximum time savings.

Ingredients

FOR CAKE

  • 5 c. all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp. plus 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 1/4 tsp. salt

Directions

Step 1

In large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Store in airtight container until ready to use, up to 3 months.
